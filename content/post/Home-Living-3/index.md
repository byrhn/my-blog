## The Reality Check (What Reviews Won't Tell You)

### 1. The Subscription "Tax" (Ring Protect Plan)
**Critical:** Without a subscription, you only get **Live View**. You cannot save or review recorded videos.
* **Cost:** Expect to pay a small monthly fee (starts around the price of a coffee) per device.
* **Our Take:** Factor this into your annual cost. If you refuse monthly fees, this doorbell loses 80% of its value.

### 2. Battery Life Reality vs. Marketing Claims
Ring advertises "months" of battery life, but real-world results vary dramatically:
* **Low-traffic areas:** Solid performance.
* **High-traffic areas:** Drains much faster (3-4 weeks).
* **Cold climates:** Battery drains 30-40% faster in freezing temps.
* **Pro Tip:** We highly recommend grabbing a spare **Quick Release Battery Pack** so you can hot-swap without downtime.

### 3. Recharging Requires Removal
To charge, you must use the special security screwdriver to remove the faceplate and battery. This means your doorbell is offline during charging unless you have a spare battery.
