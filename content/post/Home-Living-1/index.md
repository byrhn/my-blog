## The Frustrations & Solutions (The Bad)
*Honesty time with solutions:*

* **The Learning Curve (Don't Soak It!):** Beginners often leave the sofa too wet.
    * **The Trick:** Spray once, scrub, and then do **3 slow passes with suction only** (trigger released). This dries the fabric much faster.
* **Cleaning the Hose:** Cleaning the machine used to be annoying.
    * **The Hack:** Fill a clean bowl with water and suction it up with the machine. This "hydro-rinses" the hose instantly.
* **It is Loud:** It sounds like a full-sized vacuum. Don't use it while the baby is sleeping.
